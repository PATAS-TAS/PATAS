# Outcome

PATAS now has a bounded first-customer outreach roster with named recipients,
official contact paths, exact first-touch copy, qualification follow-up, paid
offer sequencing, and explicit skip cases. No public candidate is mislabeled as
ready for the $500 offer, and no external message has been sent.

# Evidence

- The first wave separates five plausible end-user buyers from three reusable platform/channel contacts and keeps weak payers or adapter mismatches in interview-only status.
- Every first-touch message cites one public operating fact, asks one currentness or channel question, and requests no customer data, credentials, database dump, NDA, or call.
- The roster preserves the paid-pilot gate: current recurring pain, economic threshold, native-tool gap, operational rule destination, retained abuse-plus-legitimate history, processing authority, and budget owner.
- Forum solicitation restrictions are enforced: n8n uses a professional off-forum channel and IDM remains research-only until a permitted business route exists.
- Sonar, Screenly, generic OSS forums, and product documentation are explicitly rejected as paid leads rather than used to pad the pipeline.
